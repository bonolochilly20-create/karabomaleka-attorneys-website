About the Firm

Established · Centurion, Gauteng

A Modern Practice
Built on Principle

Karabo Maleka Attorneys Inc is a boutique South African law practice committed to one principle above all: your interests come first.

Founded on the belief that every client deserves direct access to a qualified attorney — not a paralegal or junior associate — we provide clear, frank legal advice backed by thorough preparation and robust courtroom advocacy. Whether you face a family crisis, a criminal charge, an unfair dismissal, or a complex estate matter, we meet you where you are and guide you through every step of the process.

Based in Amberfield, Centurion, and serving clients throughout Gauteng, we combine the responsiveness of a boutique firm with the competency of a well-resourced practice.

How We Work

Your Journey From First Call
to Final Resolution

  1. 01

    Initial Consultation

    We begin with a confidential consultation — in person or by phone — to understand your matter fully. You receive an honest assessment, a clear outline of your options and realistic expectations.

  2. 02

    Strategy & Engagement

    Once engaged, we develop a bespoke legal strategy. Fees are discussed transparently, documentation is gathered, and your matter is registered with the firm's formal systems.

  3. 03

    Active Representation

    We manage all correspondence, court filings, negotiations, and appearances. You are kept informed at every milestone with clear, jargon-free updates.

  4. 04

    Resolution & Follow-Through

    We pursue the best possible outcome and ensure full implementation of any order, agreement or settlement — your matter is closed only when you are satisfied.
